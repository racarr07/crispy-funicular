December 12, 2020

I've been starting to see album(s) of the year posts on the Instagram vinyl accounts I follow, so I thought it would be a good time to give my thoughts on this topic. My album of the years is Spanish Love Songs Brave Faces Everyone. This album came out in the beginning of the year. At the time, I didn't think much of it even though I did preorder it. I listened to the album over the summer. I had put it off listening to it until I listened to Kick on Spotify. After that, I kept reminding myself to listen to it and kicking myself for not listening to it when it came out. As soon as I finished listening to it I knew it was going to be my album of the year (even with contender Lament coming out late in the year). To me the album has summed up the year pretty well-a feeling of hopelessness and desperation brought about due to COVID which amplified normal struggles like depression, lonliness, and debt. I felt it was a higer energy version of The Menzingers Good Things . My favorite tracks of this album are Losers and Beach Front Property.
